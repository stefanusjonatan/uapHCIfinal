HISTORY OF M&T

FASHION IS PASSION

M&T was first founded by a young man from Jakarta, Indonesia since 2002. He is Gaben. At a young age, Gaben already has a curiosity about someone's fashion. Appearing as a cheerful person makes Gaben realize that fashion can make everyone happy. Therefore, the idea was born to make a breakthrough by selling the clothes he made to people around him, including his parents. Seeing the excellent response from everyone, Gaben decided to set up his first small factory.

Being known and liked by many does not make Gaben safe from the fear of failure with a move that is very wild and yet promising for his time. However, he managed to get through all of this with the support of his parents and siblings. His podruk was getting more and more sought after, therefore, it was decided that Gaben would set up his first big business. 2 years have passed, Gaben's efforts often find a dead end when there is no new design. Therefore, Gaben's step is to recruit young designers to form a trend that can be enjoyed by all groups. Until 2006, Gaben had 4 designers in his company. Since then, Gaben's company has grown and grown in Indonesia.

Gaben also needs a brand name that can represent their company. Finally came the name M & T. The name M&T itself is the initials of his parents, namely Marie and Toni. That way, M&T products are getting bigger in Indonesia and even worldwide. The first store that opened overseas was in the United States, where at that time an investor was on vacation in Indonesia, and was interested in M&T products, from there the relationship between M&T and the investor was established. Finally in 2008, the first international store was formed in the United States. Since then, more and more people are interested in this M&T product, so the opening of this store is accompanied by plans to open stores in Australia and Germany. Not to forget, M&T is also trying to develop its company throughout Indonesia.

Gaben Logan Newell

Founder of M&T Fashion Company
